Photography Blog

Welcome to my photo blog! This is a new project for me and I'm still in the process of organizing old photos into blog posts with context. There may not be much content yet, but I have plenty of photos to share!

This is my photography blog, where I will share and describe some of my favorite photos. Almost all of my photography is "macro", meaning high-magnification photos of very small things.

The most common subject of my blog is spiders. Some posts will be be about insects, gastropods, or microbes. Actually, hymenoptera (wasps, ants, and bees) and coleoptera (beetles) are some of my favorite creatures. But spiders make a better hobby for several reasons: they are common in wide variety, they are badly misunderstood, and identification resources are much more widely available.

Equipment

Most of my photos were taken on a Pixel 3 or Pixel 5. Some of those were additionally taken through a 10x magnification hand lens - these are typically distinguishable by a very shallow focus (where most of the subject will be out of focus and the background will have pronounced bokeh).

I also recently upgraded with a Sony a7 II mirrorless digital camera & FE 90mm F2.8 Macro G OSS lens. Switching from a smartphone to an advanced camera has a significant learning curve, though, and I have limited time for photography, so I can't guarantee a huge quality jump in the a7 photos just yet.
