a biography

Charles Olympia Cole is a meaningless phrase nearly everywhere in the universe. However, it is currently the name of at least one live human in Texas, Earth.

That person writes programs in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other unnatural languages both for entertainment and professionally. These programs are roughly as complex as a home budget or a role-playing game's instruction manual, and we can neither appreciate nor resent being created.
