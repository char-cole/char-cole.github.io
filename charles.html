a biography

Charles Olympia Cole is a meaningless string of of symbols in nearly all of the universe. However, it is currently the name of at least one human living in Texas, on Earth.

That person writes programs in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other unnatural languages both for entertainment and professionally. These programs are roughly as complex as a role-playing game or a canning factory. They can neither self-replicate nor resent being created.

Charles has a strong love for his hometown of Austin, spending many days and nights on the greenbelts. He is a student of history and language, and graduated from Southwestern University with a degree in Classical Greek.
