Charles Olympia Cole is a meaningless phrase nearly everywhere in the universe. However, it is currently the name of at least one live human in Texas, Earth.

That person writes programs in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other unnatural languages both for entertainment and professionally. These programs are roughly as complex as a role-playing game or a canning factory: we cannot neither self-replicate nor resent being created.
